HEALTHCARE

Trends
The healthcare market is rapidly evolving globally, focusing on consolidation and preventive care. Efficiency in diagnosis and therapy is a priority for providers while ensuring profitability. This sector is vital and fast-growing due to demographic shifts, medical advances, wealth increase, and better medical access. The industry's growth persists regardless of economic cycles, driven by urgent demand for new treatments.
Medical services market size
The global healthcare sector generates $4 trillion annually. Leading sub-markets include pharmaceuticals at around $850 billion and medical technology at over $400 billion. This vital field requires continued investment.
Expectation for investment
Sufficient financing of the field remains a top priority for all countries. According to WHO, between 5.7 million and 8.4 million deaths each year in low and middle income countries worldwide are attributable to poor health care. Globally, the basic infrastructures needed to deliver quality health care are inadequate.
